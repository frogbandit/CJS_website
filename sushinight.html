Come to ALL YOU CAN EAT sushi with CJS!!

On Friday, November 11th, come make your own sushi with friends while enjoying performances by Columbia groups.

Sushi Night is divided into two hour-long sessions that can each seat 80 people.

This year's theme is Japanese temples and shrines! So get excited for beautiful decorations and fun trivia on Japanese shrines and temples!!

Get your tickets before they sell out!! It's the only way to be sure you can attend!

Session 1: 6:00-7:00pm
Session 2: 7:00-8:00pm
Where: Lerner Broadway Room (next to Piano Lounge)
Ticket Prices: $5 online, $7 at the door
