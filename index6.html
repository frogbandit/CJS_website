About Columbia Japan Society

CJS was founded in order to introduce aspects of Japanese culture to members of the Columbia community.



In the past, we have held annual events such as Sushi Night, Curry in a Hurry, Movie Night, and Matsuri, as well as new events each year! Learn more about us!
