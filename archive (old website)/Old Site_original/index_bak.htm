Welcome to
Columbia Japan Society
(Japanese Version)

Columbia Japan Society was founded in order to introduce aspects of Japanese culture to members of the Columbia community. In past years, we have held events such as Sexy Sushi Night, Curry in a Hurry, Movie Night, and Matsuri (Japanese Festival).

You do NOT need to be Japanese to join CJS – all you need is an interest in anything Japanese! So let us know if you would like to be part of the CJS family and welcome to Columbia Japan Society!
