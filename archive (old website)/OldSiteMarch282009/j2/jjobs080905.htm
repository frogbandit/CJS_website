Pacific Bridge Medical Asian Business Associate Position 08.09.05 
       

 
Pacific Bridge Medical (PBM) is an international consulting firm focused on
Asia. The company was formed in 1988 to help American medical companies cope 
with a number of basic problems in doing business in Asia. Since then, PBM 
has assisted over 150 companies with business development, marketing strategy, 
and regulatory issues in Asia. This is an outstanding opportunity to be a key 
player at the company and to learn a great deal. All the work done at PBM 
involves Asia and business in Asia, so this is an excellent chance for those 
interested in the region to put their knowledge to practice.


JOB DESCRIPTION:

1) Research and Writing
     a. Research for PBM's outside publications
     b. Write topical articles on medical regulatory issues in Asia

2) Audio Conferences
     a. Coordinating speakers and participants
     b. Arranging for audio conference services
     c. Some research and presentation writing

3) Consulting Projects
     a. Work on projects and reports for PBM's medical businesses


CANDIDATE REQUIREMENTS:

1) 3.4 GPA or higher

2) Strong interest in Asia

3) Hardworking, dedicated, loyal, and able to multi-task

4) Extremely detail oriented

5) Excellent writing skills

6) Fluent English

7) Japanese-English bilingual a plus

8) No previous medical experience required



The Associate will work in a fast paced environment with abundant room 
to grow and perform. College graduates or candidates with several years
experience are welcome to apply. Compensation will be commensurate
with experience and will range from $32,000 to $40,000. Interested
candidates should review our website, http://www.pacificbridgemedical.com 
to learn more about our business and publications.

Please send your resume and detailed cover letter, including an explanation
of why you are interested in this position, to Shawna Lepage at 
slepage@pacificbridge.com. Resumes without cover letters will not be 
considered. No phone calls please.
